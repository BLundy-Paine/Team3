Each page of this site highlights a poet that has left a cultural impact in California. They all lived in various time periods and influenced diverse subcultures including music, feminist, and literary culture. Their impact is far-reaching and intersect here, in Monterey, CA.

Adrienne Rich

George Sterling

John Steinbeck

Vincent Randazzo
